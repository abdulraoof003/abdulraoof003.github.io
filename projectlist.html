Project List

HBOS - I designed and developed two systems which extracted data from the Customer Marketing Database. One of these passed data into a Customer Credit Scoring system (TRIAD) purchased by the Halifax from Fair Issacs Ltd. This enabled Credit scores to be assigned to Customers holding specific product types. The other system produced data which enabled potential Money Laundering activity which might be taking place within Halifax to be identified.
